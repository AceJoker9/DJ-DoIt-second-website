About DJ DoIT

Growing up and cleaning on Saturday mornings while listening to old school music has always had an impact on me.

  • I only work with customers who want an engaging personality who can command a room and create a pleasant atmosphere where everyone can let loose.
  • Behind the scenes, I work hard to control the timeline's speed, keep all other vendors on track, and guide everyone through any real-time changes to the order of events.
  • I am well-versed in a wide range of musical genres, so a party may contain something for everyone.

I founded and ran my own recording studio when I was a teenager. My own music was produced and engineered. I want to do my part to carry on Detroit's musical legacy in my own unique way.

Frequently Asked Questions

What should the customer know about your pricing (e.g., discounts, fees)?

Our pricing will vary based on your needs, location and desired package.

What advice would you give a customer looking to hire a provider in your area of work?

Make sure they understand who you are and your expectations. The more you tell me up front the more I'm able to handle it all when it's time to celebrate - letting you catch a break and enjoy.

What is your typical process for working with a new customer?

I want to get to know you. Whether it be a phone call or a cup of coffee, I want to know exactly what you envision for your event and how I can take care of all the entertainment so that you have one less thing to worry about. I will provide an event planning form for the client to complete and then will review it together. After that, I take care of the rest.

What types of customers have you worked with?

I regularly provide DJ services for schools, weddings, birthday parties, graduation parties, anniversary parties, corporate events and more.

Describe a recent project you are fond of. How long did it take?

As a musician ourselves, I find a lot of enjoyment in creating mixes just for fun in my spare time. A typical mix takes anywhere from 2 - 4 hours. Starting with choosing the song list to making the beats blend perfectly, and ending with a masterpiece.
